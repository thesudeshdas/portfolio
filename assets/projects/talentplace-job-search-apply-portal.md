Built the frontend for TalentPlace.ai's job-search and apply portal.

The product included job listings, search, filters, app scaffolding, an
end-to-end apply flow, application summaries, candidate-facing views and chart
visualizations. Backend ownership stayed outside my scope; this work focused
on frontend product delivery and API integration from the frontend side.

The portal was part of a broader TalentPlace.ai career product suite alongside
career profiles, resume templates and account flows.
