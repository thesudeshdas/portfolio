A native macOS menu bar app that protects your eyes during extended screen time using the 20-20-20 rule — every 20 minutes, take a 20-second break and look at something 20 feet away.

Built entirely with Swift and SwiftUI with zero external dependencies. Features configurable work/break intervals, a full-screen overlay with circular countdown on all connected monitors, and 6 visual themes with adjustable opacity and blur.

The standout feature is Smart Pause — it automatically detects screen recordings (Loom, OBS, CleanShot), video calls (Zoom, Meet, FaceTime via camera usage), and fullscreen apps (presentations, games), pausing reminders until the activity ends.

Includes strict mode (disables skip/postpone), daily break counter, launch-at-login support, and pre-break warning notifications. Prepared for open source with full documentation, architecture guide, and contribution guidelines.
