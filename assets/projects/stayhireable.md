StayHireable is a career productivity tool for engineers who want to stay ready
before a job search starts.

It combines a Career Vault, work journal, resume bullet evidence, project
history and job application tracking into one workflow. The goal is to turn
day-to-day engineering work into searchable proof: what was shipped, why it
mattered, what skills were used and how it should show up in a resume or
interview.

Current focus areas include structured experience tracking, journal-backed
resume bullets, application confirmation capture and career profile readiness.
